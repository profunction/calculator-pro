Frequently Asked Questions - Unit Converter

How do I convert Fahrenheit to Celsius?

Use the formula C = (F - 32) x 5/9. For example, 68 F = (68 - 32) x 5/9 = 20 C. Water freezes at 32 F (0 C) and boils at 212 F (100 C).

How many feet are in a meter?

One meter equals approximately 3.28084 feet. To convert meters to feet, multiply by 3.28084. To convert feet to meters, divide by 3.28084. This exact conversion factor has been standardized internationally.

What is the difference between metric and imperial systems?

The metric system (SI) is based on powers of 10 and is used by most countries. The imperial system (used in the US, Liberia, and Myanmar) uses different base units like inches, feet, pounds, and gallons with various conversion factors between them.

How do I convert volume units for cooking?

Common cooking conversions: 1 cup = 16 tablespoons = 48 teaspoons = 8 fluid ounces = 236.6 ml. 1 tablespoon = 3 teaspoons = 15 ml. For baking accuracy, use weight measurements (grams) rather than volume when possible.

Why do some unit conversions use decimals instead of fractions?

Decimal conversions are more precise and universal. Fractions like 1/2, 1/4, 1/8 are convenient for everyday use but limit precision. Scientific and engineering applications always use decimal representations for accuracy.
