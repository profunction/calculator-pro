Car Lease Calculator — Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate a car lease payment?

Use our free Car Lease Calculator above. Enter the car price, down payment, lease term, and interest rate. The calculator shows your monthly lease payment, total cost, and comparison with buying outright.

What is the difference between leasing and buying a car?

Leasing means you pay for the car's depreciation during the lease term plus interest — you don't own the car. Buying means you pay the full price (often with a loan) and own the car after the loan is paid. Leasing typically has lower monthly payments but no asset ownership.

How is Malaysia road tax calculated?

Malaysia road tax is based on engine capacity (cc). Higher engine capacity = higher road tax. Our calculator automatically computes the road tax based on the engine size you enter, for both petrol and diesel vehicles.

What factors affect my car lease payment?

Car lease payments depend on: car price (capitalized cost), down payment, lease term length, interest rate (money factor), and the car's estimated residual value at lease end. A higher down payment or longer term reduces monthly payments.

Should I lease or buy a car?

Leasing is better if you want lower monthly payments and change cars frequently. Buying is better if you want to own the car long-term and avoid mileage restrictions. Our calculator shows a side-by-side comparison to help you decide.
