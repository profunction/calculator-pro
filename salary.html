Salary Calculator — Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate my take-home pay?

Use our free Salary Calculator above. Enter your gross salary and select your country. The calculator automatically applies the correct tax rates, social security contributions, and other deductions to show your net take-home pay.

What is EPF in Malaysia?

EPF (Employees Provident Fund) is Malaysia's retirement savings scheme. Employees contribute 11% and employers contribute 13% (for salaries below RM5,000) or 12% (above RM5,000). Our calculator automatically computes EPF deductions.

What is SOCSO/EIS in Malaysia?

SOCSO (Social Security Organization) provides social protection for Malaysian workers. EIS (Employment Insurance System) provides temporary financial assistance if you lose your job. Both are mandatory deductions calculated automatically in our salary calculator.

How is US federal income tax calculated?

US federal income tax uses progressive tax brackets — higher income portions are taxed at higher rates. Our calculator applies the correct 2024 tax brackets, standard deduction, and FICA (Social Security 6.2% + Medicare 1.45%) to compute your take-home pay.

Can I compare salaries across countries?

Yes! Our Salary Calculator supports multiple countries including Malaysia, US, China, Singapore, and EU countries. Switch between countries to see how the same gross salary translates to different take-home pay amounts.
